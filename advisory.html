Advisory Services

The Advisory Services practice provides comprehensive financial, economic, and strategicadvice to governments and private companies with complex business problems or disputes with respect to improving the extractive minerals value chain. The knowledge, depth and expertise of our extractive minerals industry specialized teams is unmatched by any other financial institution in Africa. Our range of specialized advisory services in mission critical areas of corporate and business finance include:

Capital Raising

We assist mining companies in securing the necessary funding for their projects through a variety of financial instruments, including equity, debt, and hybrid financing options. Our extensive network of investors and financial institutions ensures that clients receive competitive financing solutions tailored to their specific needs.

Project Development

AEMBANK supports the initial stages of mining projects by providing expertise in planning, feasibility studies, and securing the necessary permits and approvals. Our goal is to ensure that projects are viable and sustainable, contributing to the economic growth of the region.

Policy & Technical

Our Policy & Technical services offer strategic advice on regulatory compliance, environmental standards, and best practices in the mining industry. We work closely with governments and private sector clients to develop policies that promote sustainable development and operational excellence.

Restructuring

In times of financial distress or organizational change, AEMBANK provides restructuring services to help companies streamline operations, reduce debt, and improve financial performance. Our team of experts guides clients through the process of organizational change, ensuring stability and long-term success.

Privatisations

We facilitate the transfer of ownership of state-owned mining assets to private investors, helping to attract foreign investment and enhance operational efficiency. Our privatization services include valuation, transaction advisory, and post-transaction support to ensure smooth transitions and optimal outcomes.

Asset Disposal

When companies need to divest non-core assets, our Asset Disposal services provide comprehensive support, from asset valuation to identifying potential buyers and negotiating favorable terms. We help clients maximize the value of their disposals while minimizing operational disruptions.

Divestments & Unbounding

Our Divestments & Unbundling services assist clients in selling off parts of their business or separating distinct business units to unlock value and improve focus. We provide strategic advice and execution support to ensure that divestments and unbundling processes are efficient and value-accretive.

IPOs

AEMBANK offers support for initial public offerings (IPOs), guiding companies through the complex process of going public. We help clients prepare for the IPO, navigate regulatory requirements, and attract investors, ensuring a successful market debut and ongoing access to capital markets.
