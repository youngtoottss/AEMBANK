All Services

At the heart of our mission, we cater to a diverse customer profile including Governments, Multinationals/Local Corporates, and Indigenous Extractive Minerals Operators. Our service offerings are designed to address the unique needs of these sectors through specialized practices in Advisory Services, Development Finance, and Trade Finance. Each of these services is crafted to enhance the value chain and promote sustainable economic growth within Africa's extractive minerals industry.

Advisory Services

The Advisory Services practice provides comprehensive financial, economic, and strategic advice to governments and private companies with complex business problems or disputes with respect to improving the extractive minerals value chain. The knowledge, depth and expertise of our extractive minerals industry specialized teams is unmatched by any other financial institution in Africa.

Development Finance

The Development Finance practice adopts a transformative impact-financing approach towards promoting infrastructure investment and local economic development that retains value within the African continent. Working with governments, domestic banks and local businesses, the Bank will design, pilot and test out financing mechanisms and business models in both public and private sectors that support locally designed public investments and revenue generating capital investment projects.

Trade Finance

Trade finance unit offers a comprehensive range of trade finance programs to finance, and facilitate finance, that will support African trade with the rest of the world.
